What Are Gold and Gems Used For?

  • Gold: Used to upgrade your cards, buy cards from the shop, and participate in special events or challenges.
  • Gems: Used to speed up chest unlocks, buy special offers, enter challenges or tournaments, and purchase cosmetics.

Understanding how to acquire these currencies without spending money is essential for long-term success in Clash Royale.

1. Complete the Free Rewards on the Trophy Road

  • Unlock chests, cards, Gold, and occasionally Gems by progressing in the Trophy Road.
  • Milestone rewards often include hundreds or thousands of Gold.
  • Gems are occasionally awarded at higher trophy milestones.

Tip: Consistent wins and climbing the ladder steadily will maximize these rewards.

2. Open Free Chests Daily

  • Free Chests: Automatically available and refresh over time.
  • Crown Chests: Unlock by collecting 10 crowns from battles.
  • Seasonal Chests: Available during events and can offer enhanced rewards, including Gems.

Tip: Open all available chests daily to accumulate resources steadily.

3. Utilize the Pass Royale (Free Tier)

  • Earn Free Gold and Cards each time you level up on the free track.
  • Get trade tokens and occasionally free Gems.
  • Access free cosmetics and Emotes.

Tip: Always complete daily and weekly quests to progress faster on the free Pass Royale track.

4. Participate in Clan Wars

  • Win battles to contribute to your clan's total and unlock war chests full of Gold.
  • Earn trade tokens to acquire cards faster without spending Gold.
  • The better your clan performs, the bigger the rewards.

Tip: Join an active clan that regularly participates in wars to maximize your rewards.

5. Complete Daily Quests and Weekly Challenges

  • Win a certain number of battles.
  • Use specific card types or play in particular modes.

Tip: Prioritize quests with higher Gold/Gem rewards and fit them into your normal gameplay routine.

6. Take Advantage of Special Events and Tournaments

  • Look for free or discounted entries in Classic and Grand Challenges, and Global Tournaments.
  • Earn chests, Gems, Gold, and exclusive rewards based on performance.
  • Participate in community-run or official events offering Gems as rewards.

8. Trade Cards in Your Clan

Trading cards with clanmates can indirectly save you Gold by helping you acquire needed cards faster, reducing the amount of Gold spent upgrading irrelevant cards.

Tip: Use Trade Tokens strategically to get cards you frequently use and upgrade your main deck.

9. Be Patient and Strategic with Chest Management

  • Always have a chest unlocking in the queue.
  • Focus on higher-tier chests like Magical, Epic, or Giant chests for more Gold/cards.
  • Avoid using Gems to speed unlocks unless you have extras.

Final Tips for Efficient Free-to-Play Farming

  • Stay informed: Follow official channels for free events and giveaways.
