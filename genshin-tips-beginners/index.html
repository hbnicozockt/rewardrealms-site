15 Tips for Beginners in Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is one of the most popular free-to-play action RPGs worldwide, combining beautiful open-world exploration with fast-paced combat and a deep character system. New players are often overwhelmed by the sheer size of the world, the amount of resources, and the complex mechanics. If you're just starting out, this guide will walk you through everything you need to know â€” from combat basics to character building, exploration, and resource management. With the right strategies, you'll progress faster, avoid common mistakes, and enjoy the game much more.

1. Creating Your Account

To start playing Genshin Impact, you need to set up a miHoYo (HoYoverse) account.

  • Steps: Download Genshin Impact on PC, mobile, or console. Sign up using your email or social media account. Verify your email to secure your account.
  • Tips: Link your account across platforms to enable cross-save. Choose a memorable nickname. Set up two-factor authentication for extra security.

2. Understanding the Basics

When you first log in, you're introduced to the Traveler, your main character. From there, you'll unlock more characters and begin exploring the world of Teyvat.

  • Important beginner elements: Adventure Rank (AR) - determines your progress and unlocks new features. World Level - increases difficulty but also improves loot quality. Resin - a resource used to claim rewards from bosses and domains. Primogems - the premium currency used for Wishes (character and weapon pulls).

3. Exploring the Open World

Exploration is one of the highlights of Genshin Impact.

  • Activate teleport waypoints as soon as you see them.
  • Climb mountains and glide to discover hidden areas.
  • Open every chest â€” they contain Primogems, materials, and weapons.
  • Collect Anemoculi and Geoculi to upgrade your Statue of the Seven for stamina boosts.

Pro tip: Always keep some food items ready for stamina recovery during climbing or swimming.

4. Combat Basics

Combat in Genshin Impact is dynamic and based on elemental reactions.

  • Seven elements in the game: Pyro (fire), Hydro (water), Cryo (ice), Electro (lightning), Anemo (wind), Geo (earth), Dendro (nature).
  • Elemental reactions: Hydro + Cryo = Freeze, Pyro + Hydro = Vaporize (bonus damage), Electro + Pyro = Overloaded (explosion damage), Dendro + Hydro = Bloom (creates cores that can explode).

Tip: Always build teams that combine different elements for maximum damage.

5. Building a Team

You can bring up to four characters into your party.

  • Beginner-friendly team roles: Main DPS - the character who deals most of the damage. Sub DPS - provides additional damage and elemental reactions. Support - buffs allies or debuffs enemies. Healer/Shield - keeps the team alive during tough fights.
  • Example for early game: Traveler (support damage), Amber (Pyro ranged DPS), Kaeya (Cryo sub DPS), Barbara (healer).

6. Leveling Characters and Weapons

It's tempting to level up every character you get, but resources are limited.

  • Focus on 1â€“2 main damage dealers first.
  • Save resources for characters you really like or that fit your playstyle.
  • Use Enhancement Ores for weapons, not random drops.
  • Ascend characters at AR 20, 40, and 50 to unlock higher level caps.

Tip: Don't spread resources too thin â€” invest in a core team.

7. Managing Resin

Resin is one of the most important resources in Genshin Impact.

  • Daily cap: 160 Resin (recharges slowly).
  • Use it on bosses, domains, and ley lines.
  • Don't waste Resin on low-value activities.
  • Beginner priority for Resin: World Bosses for character ascension materials. Domains for artifacts (later in the game). Weapon materials.

8. Farming Primogems

Primogems are essential for Wishes (pulling new characters and weapons).

  • Ways to earn Primogems: Story quests and Archon quests. Daily commissions (60 per day). Spiral Abyss (endgame challenge). Events and achievements. Chests and exploration.

Tip: Save Primogems for limited-time banners rather than standard wishes.

9. Artifact Basics

Artifacts are gear that boost your stats.

  • Don't worry about 5-star artifacts early â€” focus on 3-star and 4-star sets.
  • Prioritize main stats over substats in the beginning.
  • Recommended sets for early game: Berserker, Instructor, and Adventurer.
  • At higher AR, farm domains for stronger sets like Gladiator's Finale, Noblesse Oblige, and Crimson Witch of Flames.

10. Adventure Rank Progression

Your Adventure Rank unlocks new content:

  • AR 12: Daily commissions become available.
  • AR 20: Battle Pass and Spiral Abyss unlock.
  • AR 25: Domains of higher difficulty appear.
  • AR 30+: Stronger bosses and higher artifact drops.

Tip: Focus on completing story quests and commissions to raise AR efficiently.

11. Daily Routine for Beginners

To make steady progress, follow a daily routine:

  • Complete 4 daily commissions.
  • Spend all your Resin.
  • Farm local specialties for ascensions.
  • Collect ore and craft enhancement materials.
  • Explore one new area for chests and waypoints.

12. Co-Op Mode

Genshin Impact allows multiplayer co-op after AR 16.

  • Benefits of co-op: Farm bosses faster with friends. Share resources and strategies. Learn new character combos.

Tip: Communication is key â€” coordinate which elements your team brings to maximize reactions.

13. Common Beginner Mistakes

Avoid these pitfalls:

  • Leveling too many characters at once.
  • Wasting Primogems on the standard banner.
  • Ignoring artifacts and weapon upgrades.
  • Spending Resin on low-level domains.
  • Not saving food and healing items.

14. Free-to-Play (F2P) Friendly Tips

Genshin Impact can be enjoyed fully without spending money.

  • F2P strategies: Claim all daily login rewards. Focus on strong 4-star characters like Xiangling, Xingqiu, and Bennett. Use the Stardust Exchange to buy Fates each month. Take advantage of all limited-time events.

15. Advanced Beginner Tips

Once you've mastered the basics, try these:

  • Practice animation canceling to increase DPS.
  • Learn enemy attack patterns for dodging.
  • Farm materials in advance for upcoming character banners.
  • Experiment with different team comps for Spiral Abyss.
  • Watch content creators and guides for advanced rotations.

16. Long-Term Goals

To keep improving in Genshin Impact:

  • Build a strong roster of characters for multiple team setups.
  • Farm artifact sets for endgame optimization.
  • Complete the Spiral Abyss floors for Primogems.
  • Collect all Archons and complete the storyline.
  • Explore every corner of Teyvat to discover hidden secrets.

Conclusion

Genshin Impact is a game that rewards patience, exploration, and smart resource management. Beginners should focus on building a balanced team, understanding elemental reactions, and using resources wisely. Don't rush â€” enjoy the journey through Teyvat, learn from your mistakes, and gradually grow stronger.

With these beginner tips, you'll be well on your way to building powerful characters, clearing tough bosses, and enjoying everything Genshin Impact has to offer. Whether you play casually or aim to master every mechanic, this guide gives you the foundation for long-term success.
