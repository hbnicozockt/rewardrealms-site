Genshin Impact: How to Earn Free Primogems & Mora – Legit and Easy!

✨ If you want to master Genshin Impact without spending a dime, this guide covers every legit way to farm Primogems and Mora efficiently – no shortcuts, just smart gameplay. Let’s get you ready for the toughest domains and banners! Genshin Impact, miHoYo’s open-world RPG sensation, combines stunning exploration, deep lore, and strategic combat with addictive gacha pulls. Millions have fallen in love with its vast world and ever-expanding story. But progression hinges on two critical currencies: • Primogems: The rare premium currency, primarily used to buy Wishes and unlock new characters, weapons, and items. • Mora: The universal in-game currency essential for upgrading characters, weapons, crafting, and more. Though you can buy both with real money, there are many legitimate and completely free ways to build up these currencies as a free-to-play (F2P) player — and this guide lays out the full roadmap.

1. ✅ Complete Your Daily Commissions Without Fail

Every day, the Adventurers’ Guild gifts you 4 commissions—each rewards:

  • 15 Primogems (60 total daily!)
  • Adventure Rank (AR) experience to level up faster
  • Valuable Mora and crafting materials

Pro Tip: Log in early and finish your commissions right away to jump-start your day’s rewards. Missing even one means lost Primogems and progress!

2. 📜 Progress Through the Main Story & Side Quests (Archon & Story Quests)

Quests are a massive source of Primogems and Mora:

  • Archon Quests (main story) give huge chunks of Primogems — sometimes 50-100+ per chapter.
  • Side quests and character story quests provide consistent Primogem drops and valuable upgrade materials.
  • Unlock new regions and domains for more resource farming.

Pro Tip: Check your quest log daily, don’t leave rewards unclaimed — these chunks add up fast and open new game features.

3. 🗺️ Explore the Open World: Chests, Secrets & Statues

Genshin’s world is packed with hidden treasures:

  • Common, Exquisite, Precious, and Luxurious chests contain Mora and sometimes Primogems.
  • Find and offer Geoculus and Anemoculus to Statues of The Seven to receive valuable rewards, including Mora and stamina boosts.
  • Defeat weekly bosses and elite mobs to gather Mora and crafting materials.

Pro Tip: Use interactive maps or online guides to mark chest locations — exploration is your friend for free rewards.

4. ⛏️ Use Your Resin Daily to Farm Bosses and Domains

Original Resin is your energy-like resource for claiming domain rewards:

  • Use it on weekly bosses to get rare ascension materials and Primogems.
  • Farm Ley Line Outcrops for Mora and character EXP.
  • Clear domains for artifacts and weapon ascension items.

Pro Tip: Resin regenerates slowly but don’t let it cap out — spend it daily for maximum farming efficiency. Use Fragile Resin wisely during double-drop events.

5. 🎉 Participate in Limited-Time Events Regularly

miHoYo hosts tons of events featuring Primogems and Mora:

  • Login events often gift 50+ Primogems.
  • Challenges and puzzles reward both currencies and unique items.
  • Special story or puzzle events sometimes unlock exclusive cosmetics or weapons.

Pro Tip: Check the in-game event tab often and join events actively. They’re some of the fastest ways to stockpile Primogems.

6. 🌟 Climb Adventure Rank and Claim Rank-Up Rewards

Leveling up your Adventure Rank unlocks quests, chests, and rewards from the Adventurers’ Guild, including Primogems.

Pro Tip: Stay focused on AR progression by completing quests, exploring, and farming domains for steady rewards and access to higher-level content.

7. 🎟️ Maximize the Battle Pass (Gnostic Hymn) — Free Track

Genshin’s battle pass offers both free and paid rewards:

  • The free track grants you Primogems, Mora, character EXP materials, and other goodies as you level up.
  • Complete daily and weekly challenges to progress quickly.

Pro Tip: Don’t ignore daily challenges — they’re the fastest way to climb the battle pass tiers on the free track.

8. 📅 Collect Login Rewards & Special Anniversary Gifts

Keep an eye on login campaigns — especially during holidays and big updates:

  • New players often receive Primogems and Mora welcome gifts.
  • Anniversary events can hand out generous bundles of both currencies.

Pro Tip: Set calendar reminders for special login campaigns to never miss free currency.

9. 🏆 Track & Claim Achievements Often

Genshin Impact’s achievement system hands out Primogems for milestones like:

  • Exploring new areas
  • Defeating bosses repeatedly
  • Completing domains and special quests

Pro Tip: Check your achievements tab regularly and claim any uncollected Primogems — these add up surprisingly fast.

10. 🌀 Challenge the Spiral Abyss for Top-Tier Rewards

The Spiral Abyss dungeon is one of the best sources for Primogems:

  • Rewards for clearing floors and earning high star ratings are generous.
  • Floors reset twice a month, allowing you to grind for Primogems repeatedly.

Pro Tip: Build strong teams with synergy, optimize your artifact builds, and keep pushing floors for consistent Primogem income.

🧠 Bonus Tips for Efficient Free-to-Play Progression

  • Save Primogems wisely: Don’t blow them on every banner. Focus on your favorite characters or limited-time banners.
  • Manage Resin efficiently: Use resin on domains and bosses with the best drops for your current needs.
  • Join communities: Follow Reddit, Discord, and Twitter for event schedules, farming guides, and giveaways.
  • Plan upgrades: Focus Mora and materials on characters and weapons you use the most.

🚀 Final Thoughts: How to Thrive in Genshin Impact as a Free Player

Patience and persistence are your best allies. By consistently completing commissions, chasing quests, managing resin, and jumping on events, you’ll steadily amass enough Primogems and Mora to compete with paid players.

Keep exploring, learning, and planning your pulls wisely — soon you’ll build a dream team and unlock the full depth of Genshin Impact’s world without spending a cent.
