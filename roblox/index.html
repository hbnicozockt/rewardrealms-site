The first and most obvious method is the Roblox Microsoft Rewards program. By signing up and completing tasks through Bing (Microsoft's search engine), users can accumulate points which can then be redeemed for Robux gift cards. It's a legit system endorsed by Microsoft and available in several regions. All you need is consistent engagement, and the points will quickly add up.

Another option is through the Roblox Premium membership. While it does cost money monthly, the membership gives you a Robux stipend every month and allows you to trade and sell items, earning Robux in return. Some users use this as a stepping stone to develop and sell their own games or items, making their Robux back and more.

Community giveaways hosted by popular Roblox YouTubers or streamers are a common way to win Robux or Roblox gift cards. These giveaways usually require you to follow, like, comment, or join a Discord server. Just be cautious and only participate in giveaways from trusted creators to avoid scams.
