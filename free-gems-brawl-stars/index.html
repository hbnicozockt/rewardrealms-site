💎 How to Get Free Gems in Brawl Stars

💎 If you read all the tips in this guide carefully, you'll 100% be able to earn free Gems consistently — and maybe even make real money along the way. Stay until the end — the last methods might surprise you! Brawl Stars is a fast-paced, competitive mobile game by Supercell — and like most freemium games, it uses a premium currency called Gems. These Gems let you unlock the Brawl Pass, buy exclusive skins, grab Mega Boxes, and more. You can always buy Gems with real money... But what if we told you there are legit ways to earn them for free — and even profit from it? Whether you're a casual player or a hardcore Brawler, this guide covers every smart strategy.

✅ 1. Everything You Can Do Inside Brawl Stars to Earn Gems

Even without any extra apps or tools, Brawl Stars itself offers several in-game opportunities to collect Gems.

🎯 Free Brawl Pass Tier

Each Brawl Pass season includes a free progression track. As you complete daily, weekly, and seasonal quests, you collect tokens that unlock rewards — including Gems.

  • You usually get 90 Gems over the course of 3–4 seasons. That’s enough to buy the paid Brawl Pass if you save up.
  • Some missions are super easy (e.g. “Win 3 games with a rare Brawler”) — do them daily.

🎁 Supercell Events

Throughout the year, Supercell runs limited-time events where you can earn free content. These may include:

  • Brawlidays
  • Lunar New Year
  • Anniversary events
  • Challenge-based events (e.g. Duels or Championship)

While rewards mostly include pins, boxes or skins, some events also offer free Gems, especially if you complete all stages.

🧠 Smart Tip: Stack Rewards

Save your free Gems over time instead of spending them immediately. Many smart players:

  • Stack Gems for multiple seasons
  • Then unlock the paid Brawl Pass
  • And use that to unlock even more Gems, skins, and content

It’s a snowball effect — all without spending a cent.

✅ 2. Google Opinion Rewards – Easy Surveys = Free Gems

This is one of the most popular and 100% legit methods — and it’s made by Google itself.

💬 How It Works:

  1. Install Google Opinion Rewards (Android or iOS)
  2. Turn on location and notifications
  3. You’ll receive short surveys (1–3 questions)
  4. Each survey pays around $0.10 to $1
  5. Credits go directly to your Google Play balance

Once you have enough credits, simply buy Gems in Brawl Stars like normal — it’s legal and instant.

🧠 Pro Tip: You get more surveys if you’re active IRL (e.g. visiting shops, cafes, or traveling).

✅ 3. More Legit Apps to Earn Gift Cards or Gems

Besides Google Rewards, here are other trusted apps that let you earn money or gift cards, perfect for buying Gems:

🕹️ Mistplay (Android)

  • Earn points (“units”) by playing new mobile games
  • The more you play, the more you earn
  • Redeem units for Google Play, Amazon, or other gift cards

🧾 Poll Pay

  • Paid surveys, similar to Google Rewards
  • Cash out via PayPal
  • Use PayPal funds to buy Google Play codes

🎥 Swagbucks Live & InboxDollars

Earn points by:

  • Watching videos
  • Taking quizzes
  • Completing offers

Rewards: Gift cards or PayPal

Great for running passively while you do other things.

🚶‍♂️ CashWalk

  • Get paid just for walking
  • App converts your steps into coins
  • Redeem coins for gift cards

💡 Bonus Tip: Run Mistplay or CashWalk in the background while doing quests in Brawl Stars!
