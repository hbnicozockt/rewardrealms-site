🎖️ Call of Duty: Warzone / CoD Mobile – Complete Free-to-Play CP Farming Guide

💸 Don’t waste real money on CP when there are 100% legal, safe, and clever ways to get it for free. If you stick to this guide, you’ll build up a CP stash season after season — just like smart F2P players do. Whether you're grinding in Warzone or playing on the go in CoD Mobile, CoD Points (CP) are the premium currency for everything: Battle Passes, operator bundles, weapon skins, crate spins, and more. But here’s the truth — you don’t have to pay to win. This guide shows you how to earn CP completely free, using proven methods that thousands of players are already using in 2025. Let’s get started.

✅ 1. Battle Pass Strategy – Recycle Your CP (CoD Mobile)

The smartest long-term method is to buy the Battle Pass once and then recycle the CP you earn from it — so you never have to pay again.

🎯 The Strategy:

  • Initial cost: 560 CP for the Battle Pass
  • Max return: 560 CP earned by reaching Tier 50
  • Result: You can buy the next season’s pass for free — forever

How to Max Out Quickly:

  • Complete daily and weekly missions
  • Join an active clan for bonus objectives
  • Play Ranked MP & BR modes for XP boosts

🔁 Repeat this each season and you’ll stay on the CP loop without spending a cent.

📌 Note: This does not apply to Warzone — that Battle Pass doesn’t give free CP unless you’ve already purchased it.

✅ 2. Limited-Time Events and Seasonal Challenges

Call of Duty runs special events all the time — and occasionally, they give out CP.

Where to Look:

  • 🎯 Daily login streaks
  • 🎉 Anniversary & holiday events
  • 🧩 Puzzle or progress-based events
  • 🔥 Special BR/MP themed missions

Example: During CoD Mobile’s 3rd Anniversary, players earned CP for logging in daily, finishing matches, and completing celebration missions.

📅 Tip: Track upcoming events via the in-game calendar or community Reddit/Discord.

✅ 3. Official Tournaments and Contests

Where CP Is the Prize:

  • 🏆 Ranked mode end-of-season rewards
  • 🎮 CoD Mobile official esports events
  • 🎯 Reddit challenges & Discord contests
  • 🖥️ Warzone: Community-based Warzone cups or kill-race tournaments

🎖️ Even small placements in bracket-based competitions sometimes get you CP or Battle Passes.

🎯 Tip: Platforms like ESL, Game.tv, and Toornament often host free-entry tourneys with rewards.

✅ 4. Google Opinion Rewards – Fast CP Without Playing

This is the #1 non-gaming way to earn CP for free.

📱 How it Works:

  • Download Google Opinion Rewards (Android)
  • Get 10–30 sec surveys every few days
  • Earn Google Play balance automatically
  • Use your balance to buy CP in CoD Mobile

💸 Typical Earnings: $2–5/month (sometimes more in big cities)

⚡ Use this to buy your first Battle Pass — then recycle from there.

✅ 5. Tapjoy Offers (CoD Mobile Only)

CoD Mobile sometimes adds Tapjoy-style offers inside the CP section.

How It Works:

  • Complete offers (like installing & leveling up in a new game)
  • Earn CP when verified

🧠 Tip: Choose offers that are free-to-complete, like watching ads, signing up for newsletters, or quick app installs.

⚠️ Avoid shady offers asking for personal data or purchases. Read user reviews!

✅ 6. Influencer Giveaways (YouTube, TikTok, Discord)

Every major season or update, creators run giveaways to boost engagement.

Where to Find Real Ones:

  • 🎥 YouTube: CoD Mobile news/content channels
  • 📱 TikTok: Skin reviewers or challenge creators
  • 🗣️ Discord: Tournament or event-based servers
  • 🐦 Twitter/X: Retweet/follow giveaways during new season drops

🎯 Tip: Follow creators like BobbyPlays, iFerg, or HawksNest, who regularly give back to their community.

⚠️ Never give your login details to join a giveaway.

✅ 7. In-Game Achievements & Clan Activity

CoD Mobile includes achievement-based rewards, and some offer direct CP.

Check these areas:

  • 🏅 Profile > Achievements tab
  • 🎖️ Ranked Match Rewards
  • 👥 Clan rewards (weekly crate or CP bonuses)

Joining an active clan boosts your passive income and makes it easier to finish weekly missions.

🔁 Some achievements also unlock crate coupons — and crates can occasionally give you CP.

✅ 8. Watch Ads for Crates or Bonuses

While not directly paying CP, CoD Mobile lets you watch ads for free crates or coupons.

🎁 Some crates have:

  • CP fragments
  • Exchangeable currency (for bundles)
  • Skins you can convert later

📌 Tip: Check the Featured or Free tabs in the store daily.

✅ 9. Xbox & PlayStation Users: Microsoft Rewards (Warzone)

If you're on console, use Microsoft Rewards to earn CP indirectly.

How:

  • Complete daily Bing searches
  • Do Xbox Game Pass quests
  • Earn points, redeem for Xbox gift cards
  • Use gift cards to buy CP or the Battle Pass in Warzone

🎯 Bonus: This also works if your Warzone account is linked to Xbox on PC.

✅ 10. Save and Spend CP Wisely

A crucial part of F2P strategy is not wasting CP.

Smart Spending Tips:

  • Prioritize the Battle Pass (best CP return)
  • Only buy time-limited skins if they offer real value (e.g., buffs, tier skips)
  • Wait for discounted bundles or return rotations

🧠 Use your CP on things that give long-term progression, not temporary flexes.
