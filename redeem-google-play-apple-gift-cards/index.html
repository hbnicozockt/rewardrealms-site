5. Common Gift Card Scams to Avoid

Scammers are creative, but most scams fall into a few common patterns.

5.1. Tech Support Scams

Someone calls pretending to be from Google or Apple, saying your account is locked and you must pay with a gift card. This is fake.

5.2. Online Marketplace Scams

Fraudsters ask for gift card codes as payment for items listed online. Once they get the code, they disappear.

5.3. Fake Prize Notifications

You receive an email or text saying you won a prize, but you must first ‚Äúverify‚Äù by providing a gift card code. Legitimate contests never work this way.

5.4. Phishing Websites

A fake website designed to look like Google or Apple prompts you to enter your gift card code. Always check the URL carefully.
