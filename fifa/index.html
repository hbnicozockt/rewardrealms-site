This guide will walk you through all the best legal and free ways to accumulate FIFA Points and Coins as a free-to-play (F2P) player.

What Are FIFA Points and Coins Used For?

  • FIFA Points: Primarily used to purchase packs, enter drafts, or buy special content in FUT.
  • Coins: Used to buy players, consumables, and other items on the FUT transfer market.

1. Play FUT Champions and Weekend League

  • FUT Champions offers competitive play with rewards including Coins, Packs, and occasionally FIFA Points.
  • High-ranked finishes yield significant Coin payouts and pack rewards.

Tip: Focus on winning as many games as possible in FUT Champions to maximize coin rewards.

2. Complete Squad Building Challenges (SBCs)

  • Completing SBCs rewards you with packs and Coins.
  • Some SBCs grant rare or special cards that can be sold for Coins.
  • Daily and weekly SBCs provide regular income opportunities.

Tip: Look for low-cost SBCs to maximize profit without overspending Coins.

3. Engage in Division Rivals

  • Matchmaking against similarly skilled players for weekly rewards.
  • Earn Coins, packs, and XP for FUT Season progress.

Tip: Consistent wins in Division Rivals grow your Coin stash over time.

4. Complete Objectives and Milestones

  • Daily, weekly, and seasonal objectives provide Coins, packs, and occasionally FIFA Points.
  • Milestones reward long-term achievements in FUT.

Tip: Check your objectives regularly and complete the easy ones for steady rewards.

5. Participate in FUT Draft Mode

  • Entry requires FIFA Points or Coins, but rewards are substantial for successful runs.
  • Occasionally, free entries or events grant access without cost.

Tip: Use Draft mode for high-value rewards without spending real money.

6. Trade Smartly on the Transfer Market

  • Buy and sell players and items based on market trends and promotions.
  • Profit from undervalued cards or smart flips.

Tip: Follow trading guides and market analysis to maximize profit margins.

7. Take Advantage of Special Events and Promotions

  • EA regularly releases seasonal events and login bonuses that offer Coins, packs, or even FIFA Points.
  • Stay updated via social media and the in-game news tab.

Tip: Be active during holidays and new season launches for maximum benefit.

8. Play Single Player Modes

  • Modes like Squad Battles against AI grant weekly rewards.
  • Perfect for more casual play while still earning Coins and packs.

9. Use Companion Apps and Web Apps

  • Manage your club, complete SBCs, and trade on the market from your phone.
  • Occasional app-exclusive offers reward Coins or packs.

10. Avoid Scams and Illegal Methods

  • Never use hacks, bots, or buy from third-party coin sellers.
  • Violations can result in permanent bans and lost progress.

Final Tips for Efficient Free-to-Play Currency Farming

  • Set daily goals for objectives and SBCs.
  • Be patient with the transfer market and learn trends.
  • Build a balanced, chemistry-strong squad for consistent wins.
  • Engage in all available game modes to diversify income.

Summary

While FIFA Points are primarily purchased with real money, Coins can be earned effectively through gameplay by competing in online modes, completing objectives, and smart trading. With dedication and smart play, free-to-play players can build a competitive FUT team and enjoy the full FIFA / EA FC experience without spending money.
